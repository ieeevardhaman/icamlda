AMLDA 2022

The International Conference on Applied Machine Learning and Data Analytics AMLDA 2022 is the premier conference in the branch of artificial intelligence. AMLDA 2022 is renowned for presenting cutting-edge research on all aspects of machine learning as well as important application areas such as healthcare and medical imaging informatics, biometrics, forensics, precision agriculture, risk management, robotics and satellite imaging. Participants at AMLDA 2022 are from academia and industrial researchers and engineers, graduate students and postdocs.

AMLDA 2022 conference is convened annually to provide a platform for knowledge exchange of the most recent scientific and technological advances in the field of applied machine learning and data analytics.

AMLDA 2022 will include Keynote Speeches, Tutorials, Workshops, Poster Presentations and Oral Presentations. On behalf of the organizing committee, we sincerely invite you to participate in this prestigious conference as authors, speakers and listeners.
